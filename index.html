About us

Tiger Tank Malaysia is a student-led initiative aimed at increasing exposure to entrepreneurial knowledge among Malaysian secondary school students through competitions, workshops and activities. Our goal is to create a new generation of founders by encouraging youth entrepreneurship and bridging the gap in opportunities between rural and urban education.

Our flagship event is the Tiger Tank Student Entrepreneur Competition. We believe this competition will be a platform for students to transform their ideas into scalable and sustainable business models that can have real world impact. Students will research, create and present business models that solve pressing real world problems.

Students will also get the experience to receive feedback from seasoned entrepreneurs on their business ideas, network with like-minded individuals and exchange insights and creative ideas. This competition is not like any other, it is an accessible platform that drives growth and creates opportunities for the Malaysian youth.

Vision

  • Impact

Why join the Tiger Tank Student Entrepreneur Competition?

  • Low barrier of entry

    Students without prior experience are encouraged to join — training videos, articles and workshops will be prepared to equip students with the entrepreneurial mindset and skills necessary to perform in this competition.

  • Networking opportunity

    Your network is your net worth — students have the opportunity to network with a community of like-minded individuals, opening room for future collaboration and long-lasting friendships.

  • Feedback from seasoned entrepreneurs

    Unlike regular innovation competitions, our competition brings the best of the best to provide their insights and opinions on the ideas of the youth based on their experiences and ventures.
